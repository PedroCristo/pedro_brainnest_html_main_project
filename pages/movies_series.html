Movies & Series

Narcos

Narcos is a highly compelling television series that stands out as one of my personal favorites. The show expertly narrates the story of Pablo Escobar from his perspective, blending elements of action and mystery to create a thrilling and engaging viewing experience.

"Prison Break" is a gripping and intense television series that remains one of my all-time favorites. The show's cleverly written script allows for each cast member and character to shine, resulting in a talented ensemble cast. However, the standouts of the show are undoubtedly the two lead actors, whose performances elevate the series to new heights of suspense and intrigue.

Bridge of Spies (film)

This movie exceeded my expectations as a truly enjoyable film. Though I anticipated a political drama centered around the Cold War, the movie delivered both compelling drama and unexpected humor. The collaboration between the Coen Brothers and Steven Spielberg proved to be a winning combination, and Tom Hanks' outstanding performance solidified the film's success.
